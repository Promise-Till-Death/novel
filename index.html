PROMISE TILL THE DEATH

A Friendship Forever


By SrinuGanesh

A group of five friends from the 1986 batch embark on a lifelong journey that stretches from their teenage years to old age. As life moves forward, circumstances slowly distance them from one another, taking each of them down different paths. Years later, fate brings them together again for one final meeting before death. The story unfolds through a timeline of memories, friendships, struggles, and emotions, with a few incidents inspired by the real situations that occurred in Andhra Pradesh in 2003.
